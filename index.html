Our Donation Plan

Donations are used to improve the condition of the most vital basic needs. These donations are distributed among food, medication, and education for the children of Africa.

Food

It costs on average just US 25 cents a day to feed a hungry child. The meals are alternated according to the nutritious value.

Medication

Children are provided with scheduled vaccinations and neccesarry medication to treat sickness as it arises.

Education

Basic education are given children to let them have enough knowledge so that they can continue their studies.
